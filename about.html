Our Projects

Ashcom Mosque Project (Maiduguri Old Bypass, Azare)

More than 70 trees were planted at the premises of Ashcom Mosque along old Maiduguri Bypass, opposite main cemetery, Azare. A small irrigation system was later added to water the trees through pvc pipes.


Katagum (Zaki) Project

13th June, 2021.

In our effort to restore our indigenous ecosystem, today some officials of KAGRAF are in Katagum (Zaki) to hand over 66 indigenous tree seedlings to the Galadiman Katagum and District Head of Katagum, Alhaji Usman Mahmood Abdullahi, who is one of the Patrons of the forum. The trees comprises of Locust (ɗorawa), Ebony (kanya) and Date Palm (dabino) seedlings. To tell you how important the project is to us, we spent more than one year to nurturing these tree seedlings which resulted in mortality more than ¾ of the earlier planted seedlings.
